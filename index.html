Top 10 Isekai Anime With Over Powered MC

Starting Life in Another World-We’re going to start things off with an absolute isekai powerhouse

BY LAZY UKITO UPDATED ON AUG 25 2023

The story follows Satoru Mikami, an ordinary 37-year-old office worker who is unexpectedly killed in a random stabbing incident. However, he finds himself reincarnated in a fantasy world as a slime, a low-level monster known for its typically weak and gelatinous nature. Despite this, Satoru gains unique abilities in his new form that allow him to analyze and mimic other creatures' abilities, as well as a form of shape-shifting.Embracing his new life as a slime, Satoru adopts the name Rimuru Tempest and begins to explore the world he's been reborn into. He quickly befriends various creatures, including powerful monsters, spirits, and humans, as he aims to create a safe and peaceful community where different species can coexist harmoniously."That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime," often abbreviated as "Slime Isekai," is a popular Japanese light novel series written by Fuse and illustrated by Mitz Vah. It falls under the isekai genre, which involves characters being transported from their normal world to a fantasy or parallel world.
